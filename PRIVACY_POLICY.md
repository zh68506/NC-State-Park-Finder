# Privacy Policy for NC State Parks

**Last Updated: December 22, 2024**

## Overview

NC State Parks ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Location Data
- **Purpose**: The app requests access to your device's location to calculate distances between your current position and North Carolina state parks.
- **Usage**: Location data is used solely to:
  - Display distances from your location to parks
  - Sort parks by proximity to your current location
  - Show your position on the map view
- **Storage**: Your location is processed locally on your device and is **never stored, transmitted, or shared** with us or any third parties.
- **Control**: You can enable or disable location access at any time through your device's Settings app. The app functions without location access, but distance-based features will be unavailable.

### Local Data Storage
The app stores the following information **locally on your device only**:
- **Favorites**: Parks you mark as favorites
- **Visited Parks**: Parks you mark as visited

This data:
- Is stored using iOS UserDefaults
- Never leaves your device
- Is not transmitted to any servers
- Is deleted when you uninstall the app

## Information We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit:
- Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
- Precise location history
- Usage analytics or statistics
- Device information
- Any other personal data

## Data Sharing

We do not share any data with third parties because we do not collect or transmit any data from your device.

## Third-Party Services

The app does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or data collection services.

## Children's Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13. The app is designed to provide information about North Carolina state parks and is suitable for all ages.

## Data Security

All app data is stored locally on your device and is protected by your device's security measures. We recommend keeping your device secure with a passcode or biometric authentication.

## Your Rights

You have complete control over the data stored by the app:
- **Location Access**: Can be enabled or disabled in device Settings at any time
- **Favorites and Visited Data**: Can be cleared by removing favorites/visited marks individually, or by uninstalling the app
- **Data Portability**: Since no data is transmitted to servers, all your data remains under your control on your device
